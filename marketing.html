MINEFIELD

Challenge your logic. Sharpen your mind. Enjoy endless fun.

Minefield brings a modern twist to the classic Minesweeper — designed to help you think deeper, play smarter, and learn as you go.

Each level is part of an endless progression, giving you infinite unique puzzles to solve. Use your logic, your instincts, and a bit of strategy to uncover safe tiles, mark mines, and keep your lives intact.

Features:

  • Endless levels with increasing challenge
  • Flagger and Magic Reveal buttons for strategic play
  • Unique lives system — learn from mistakes and keep going
  • Two board sizes: Normal (7×7) and Expert (9×9)
  • Tutorial mode for beginners
  • Light and dark mode
  • Smooth animations and a clean, distraction-free design

A calm, focused puzzle experience for curious minds.
